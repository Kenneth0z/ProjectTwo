FaQ regarding tickets and passes

-"If I want to reschedule the day that I visit the park, what should I do?"
-If you'd like to reschedule the day of your visit, contact us through either our email or phone number, found at the bottom of the page.

"Will my pearl pass subscription automatically renew upon it's experiation date?"
-No, once your pearl pass expires, you'll have to renew it by simply purchasing it again from our website.

"Can I refund my ticket or pass?"
-You may refund tickets as long as you contact us about it before the day of your initially planned visit. Pearl pass subscriptions on the other hand may not be refunded, so please be absolutely certain when purchasing them.

About Park tickets

This page is where you can view information about both our tickets and passes. Purchasing tickets alone is the cheapest way to attend the park, however the passes here on this page all provide a variety of benefits that may significantly improve the experience at Seaside Park. We recommend browsing through all of the options that we offer in order for you to decide which options you believe are the best for your upcoming visit or visits. You may purchase either physical tickets or digital tickets. Physical tickets are delivered to you through your mailing address upon completion of your purchase. Physical tickets are to be handed in at the entrance to Seaside Park on the day of your visit. Digital tickets on the other hand are delivered to you via your provided email address and may be printed to be used in the same manner as a physical ticket or can be saved as an image to be scanned at the entrance to Seaside Park on the day of your visit.
