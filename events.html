FAQ regarding our events

Q: Will I need to make an additional purchase to view or participate in certain events?
A: Not at all! All you need to do to view or participate in our events is to come at the scheduled time of the event.

Q: How do I know when and where an event takes place?
A: We do our best to keep this page updated with all of the information regarding the events we host at the park, so if you're interested in an event, check this page for any info on it.

Q: Would my pearl pass provide me with any additional benefits for any events?
A: If you have a black pearl pass, then yes! A black pearl pass will allow you to access special seating for most of the performances we hold at Thrasher Theater and for other select events as stated in the description of the black pearl pass.

About our events

We frequently host events here at Seaside Park to make the experience even more special. What we do for these events varies too. We've done live musical performances, magic acts, and even plays at our very own Thrasher Theater. For some holiday seasons or select occasions, we'll also put up seasonal decorations throughout the park and even have special items in our shops! This page is dedicated to letting you know about the schedules of our upcoming events, so if you're interested in any of them, find out more about them here!
